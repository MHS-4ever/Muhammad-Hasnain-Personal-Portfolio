2D RPG Game

  • Tech Stack: Unity, C#, 2D Game Kit, Sprite Renderer, Animator, Tilemap System

Developed an engaging 2D RPG game during my early Unity learning phase, featuring three distinct character classes: Sword Fighter, Bow Fighter, and Wizard. Each character possesses unique abilities and combat styles, allowing players to choose their preferred playstyle for an immersive gaming experience.

Implemented dynamic combat mechanics where players must eliminate enemies to earn points and progress through challenging levels. Each character's abilities—whether close combat with swords, ranged attacks with bows, or magical spells from wizards—offer strategic depth and replayability.

Designed an interactive health recovery system where players can regain lost health using collectible in-game items. This mechanic adds an additional layer of strategy, requiring players to manage their health resources wisely while navigating through intense combat scenarios.

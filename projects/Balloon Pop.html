Balloon Pop

  • Tech Stack: Unity, C#, Sprite Renderer, Animator, UI Toolkit, PlayerPrefs

Designed and developed an engaging kids' fun game where colorful balloons rise and interact with a rotating circular ring. The game combines visual appeal and quick reflex challenges to create an enjoyable experience for children.

Implemented an interactive gameplay mechanic where players tap the screen to release a balloon. If the balloon's color matches the segment of the rotating ring it collides with, the player earns a point. A mismatch results in a game over, encouraging focus and precision.

Integrated a high-score tracking system using PlayerPrefs, allowing players to challenge themselves to beat their previous best scores. The simple yet addictive gameplay loop keeps players engaged while promoting hand-eye coordination and color recognition skills.
